Madrid Abroad

Welcome to Madrid Abroad! I went to Madrid in the Spring semester of my Junior year of college to study abroad. I made this page to help answer the most common questions that someone might have if they are considering going abroad during their college experience. By clicking on the pictures below, you will be redirected to some of links that I found useful during my research process to choose a country I wanted to apply to. The other links are specific to Madrid and will give you a bit of insight into the incredible Spanish culture.

I hope you enjoy the rest of the content on the website. You will find recommendations for classes to take while at IE University, learn about the process I went through to apply to study abroad, and my journey to actually making it to Europe!

About Yael

My name is Yael Levy and I had the amazing opportunity of studying abroad in Madrid, Spain in the Spring of 2020. Although my experience got cut short because of the Covid-19 pandemic, I would still love to spread the knowledge I gained from the application process, to finding housing, and finally, my academic journey abroad for anyone that is interested in doing the same, or simply going to visit Madrid.

I am now a second semester senior and can confidently say that my education abroad experience is one that I will never forget. I had never been to Europe before and was not only able to live in Madrid, but also travel to other European countries for amazing prices.

When studying abroad, you have the choice to do an exchange program with an approved University, or to join a program with other study abroad students from all around the United States. Although more competitive, I chose to do an exchange program at IE University, where I was taking a typical second semester junior class load as a regular student at IE would take. This made the classes challenging but also rewarding and gave me the opportunity to meet students from almost every country.

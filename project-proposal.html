Project Proposal

What my website will be about:

My website will be about the experience of going abroad in Madrid as a student from the United States.

What the objective of my website is:

I want my website to help answer common questions that other students who want to go abroad in Madrid might have.

The target audience of my website is:

My target audience is students who are considering or planning to go abroad in Madrid.

The content I plan to include in each page of my website is:

I plan to include a page that details my experience from the moment I got accepted, to the moment I came home. Another page will have recommendations for classes to take at IE, the school I did my exchange program in. Another page will include restaurants that I enjoyed. Finally, I will add a page that has places to travel to in and around Madrid.

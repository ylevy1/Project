Class Schedule Abroad
Class Name Class Description from Syllabus Overall Recommendation
Business Intelligence for Startups Provides students with an introduction to business intelligence (BI) and highlights its importance to entrepreneurs as they begin developing their unique ideas and concepts for websites and mobile applications. Very interesting class if you are interested in starting your own business that requires online traffic.
Financial Modeling With Excel Financial modeling is the task of building an abstract representation of a decision-making situation. This is a model designed to represent a simplified version of the performance of a business, project, or any other marketing, human resources or commercial problem. Very useful class for learning how to model in excel. It is applicable to more than just finance.
Inequality and Economic Growth This is a course about topics related to economic inequality, growth, collective well-being, social mobility and social justice. The course covers one of the main concerns, if not the main, from actual developed and emerging societies: the necessity of achieving a higher rate of economic growth which is, at the same time, compatible with a wealth and income distribution that satisfies the requirements of justice and social cohesion from a modern democratic society. Gave me an international perspective to how economic growth affects different types of people in varying ways.
Introduction to Coding In this course you will learn programming terminology and will obtain a solid grasp of the basic mechanics of programming. This includes: Introduction to problem solving for programming (i.e., "how to think about solving the problem" including techniques such as pseudo-code or flowcharts), Understand object oriented programming and its importance in writing business software applications, You will be able to write fully functional console or GUI python programs, Basics of identifying and fixing errors by tracing the execution of code fragments Awesome class for someone interested in learning how to code in Python
Negotiation This course provides training in both the theory and practice of negotiation. The successful student will acquire the conceptual skills necessary to become a sophisticated analyst of negotiation situations. This includes a deep understanding of the underlying structure of the negotiation, as well as the psychology behind negotiation-based judgment and decision-making. Great class that is applicable to everyone in all aspects of life, even after college.
Entrepreneurship and Innovation This course is about understanding the process and complexities of creating an entrepreneurial business. Very similar to FME, so I would not recommend it for someone from Babson.
